Hamilton Boys' High School Service

This website is here to help students learn about service within the school, so more boys are motivated to help out within their community and develop a strong sense of service themselves. This website also gives an overview of the school's service award scheme, in order to help students be recognised for their service and willingness to contribute to the school and wider community.
